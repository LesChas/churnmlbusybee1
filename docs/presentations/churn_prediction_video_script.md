# Churn Prediction Explainer Video Script
## Duration: 3-5 minutes

---

## SCENE 1: THE PROBLEM (30 seconds)

### Visual:
- Show calendar with "TERMINATION NOTICE" appearing suddenly
- Revenue chart dropping

### Script:
> "Every year, we lose clients worth hundreds of thousands of dollars. 
> By the time we get the termination notice, it's often too late to save the relationship.
> What if we could see it coming weeks or months in advance?"

---

## SCENE 2: INTRODUCING THE SOLUTION (30 seconds)

### Visual:
- BusyBee logo transforms into brain/AI icon
- Dashboard preview appears

### Script:
> "Introducing BusyBee's AI-Powered Churn Prediction System.
> Using machine learning, we've trained a model on over 1,300 historical terminations
> to identify the hidden patterns that predict client churn."

---

## SCENE 3: HOW IT WORKS (60 seconds)

### Visual:
- Animated data flow diagram
- Show: Spreadsheet → AI Engine → Risk Scores

### Script:
> "Here's how it works:
> 
> First, we analyze historical data - every termination, communication log, 
> and health report from the past several years.
> 
> The AI learns what behaviors typically happen BEFORE a client leaves.
> 
> Then, it applies those patterns to your current clients,
> giving each one a risk score from 0 to 100.
> 
> Clients are categorized as Critical, High, Medium, or Low risk."

---

## SCENE 4: WHAT WE LEARNED (45 seconds)

### Visual:
- Bar chart of feature importance
- Highlight top 3 factors

### Script:
> "The model discovered some fascinating insights.
> 
> The NUMBER ONE predictor of churn? Seat count reduction.
> When a client starts reducing their team size, they're 4 times more likely to leave entirely.
> 
> Second is when a team member leaves and the client doesn't request a replacement.
> That's a major red flag.
> 
> Third is communication gaps - clients who go silent are at risk."

---

## SCENE 5: ACCURACY (30 seconds)

### Visual:
- Gauge showing 96.7%
- Simple confusion matrix animation

### Script:
> "How accurate is it?
> 
> The model has a 96.7% discrimination accuracy,
> meaning it correctly ranks risky clients higher than healthy ones almost every time.
> 
> It catches nearly 80% of clients who actually end up churning,
> giving us the early warning we need to intervene."

---

## SCENE 6: WHAT TO DO WITH IT (45 seconds)

### Visual:
- Dashboard with risk levels
- Action items appearing

### Script:
> "So what do you do with this information?
> 
> For CRITICAL risk clients: Executive outreach within 48 hours.
> Review the account, understand the issues, show them they matter.
> 
> For HIGH risk: CSP reaches out within a week. 
> Run a satisfaction survey. Address any concerns.
> 
> For MEDIUM risk: Increase your check-in frequency.
> Don't let them slip through the cracks.
> 
> For LOW risk: Continue your excellent work."

---

## SCENE 7: TRANSPARENCY (30 seconds)

### Visual:
- Show prediction with "Why?" explanation
- Checklist of transparency features

### Script:
> "Transparency is built in.
> 
> Every prediction shows exactly WHY a client is flagged.
> There are no black boxes here.
> 
> The AI recommends, but humans always make the final decision.
> It's a tool to enhance your judgment, not replace it."

---

## SCENE 8: THE BOTTOM LINE (30 seconds)

### Visual:
- Calculator showing savings
- Happy client handshake

### Script:
> "The bottom line?
> 
> If we save just 5 clients per year through early intervention,
> that's over $150,000 in protected revenue.
> 
> And more importantly, it's 5 relationships preserved,
> 5 teams that stay employed,
> and 5 success stories instead of losses."

---

## SCENE 9: CALL TO ACTION (15 seconds)

### Visual:
- Next steps checklist
- Contact information

### Script:
> "The model is trained and ready.
> 
> We're starting with a pilot program and will expand from there.
> 
> Let's work together to keep every client we can."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Tools to Create This Video:

**Option 1: Loom (Easiest)**
- Screen record the dashboard
- Add voiceover live
- Free for basic use

**Option 2: Canva (Good for animations)**
- Create animated slides
- Built-in presenter mode
- Export as video

**Option 3: PowerPoint/Google Slides**
- Create presentation
- Record with narration
- Export as video file

**Option 4: Professional (Budget)**
- Use Descript or Synthesia
- AI-generated video with your script
- More polished result

### Music Suggestions:
- Corporate upbeat (non-distracting)
- Royalty-free from YouTube Audio Library

### Duration Target:
- Ideal: 3-4 minutes
- Maximum: 5 minutes
- Executives prefer short and impactful
